Wu Jundi is a man born on 21,September 2003.He likes dash and cycling.As a track and filed fan,he has won a plenty of gold medals at school sports meetings.

In 2022,graduated from Affiliated High School of Fujian Normal University,Wu Jundi was admitted by Shandong University(SDU).

Now he is major in computer science and technology on Tsingtao campus of SDU.
